Green Sea Turtle

Some green sea turtles swim more than 2,600 kilometers (1,600 miles) to reach their spawning grounds.

  • Scientific Name: Chelonia mydas
  • Average Length: 1.5 meters
  • Average Maturity: 20 to 50 years.
  • Habitat: Tropical and subtropical seas

Green sea turtles play an essential role within the ecosystem in which they live. In the seagrass beds, the turtles feed on the seagrass by trimming only the top and leaving the roots of the plant.
